Squats

Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart or wider, and keep your hands by your sides. Slowly bend your knees as if you're sitting back into a chair. Make sure your back stays straight and your knees are over your ankles, then return to the starting position.

Push-ups

Start in a plank position with your hands on the floor, relying on the strength of your arms and shoulders. Bend your arms and lower yourself down until you're almost touching the ground, then push your body back up.

Jumping Jacks

Begin with your feet together and your arms at your sides. Jump quickly while opening your legs wide and raising your arms above your head. Jump again to return to the starting position.
